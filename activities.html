Green Initiative Activities

Watershed Based Approach

Using Watershed Based Approach, communities help to conserve biodiversity, restore ecosystems, and reduce the impact of climate change. The green initiative relies on its network of over 500 community groups to deliver its watershed based approach. At the grassroots level, Green initatives goal is to create climate resilient communities through restoration and protection of forest watersheds creating sustainable livelihoods for communities across .
