About Green Inititive

Climate change poses an existential threat to Africa's fragile ecosystems and the communities that depend on them. The Green Initiative recognizes the urgency of this crisis and is committed to mitigating its impact through sustainable practices and environmental education.

Our Mission

Our goal is to improve the environment in our community by working with area businesses, community leaders and our neighbors to create a clean and safe place to live and work. We are committed to a sustainable future and to improving the social, economic and environmental well being of the community.

Our Vision

In envisioning a sustainable future, 'Green Initiative' aspires to create a community where ecological balance, environmental consciousness, and human well-being coalesce. Our initiative seeks to foster a harmonious relationship between people and the planet, forging a path towards a greener, healthier, and more resilient community
